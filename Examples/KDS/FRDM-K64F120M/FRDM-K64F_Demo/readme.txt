This is a demo program for the FRDM-K64F board with Eclipse (Kinetis Design Studio V3.2.0).

Floppy connectors:
- yellow: Direction
- white:  Step

--------------- Ethernet   ---------------------
                          1  (SCL)
                          2  (SDA)
                          3  (AREF)
                          4  GND
                          5  PTD1 2 yellow dir
                          6  PTD3 2 white step
                          7  PTD2 3 yellow dir
                          8  PTD0 3 white step
                          9  PTC4
                          10 PTA0

1 PTB2  4 white step       1  PTC5 5 white step
2 PTB3  4 yellow dir       2  PTC7 5 yellow dir
3 PTB10 0 white step       3  PTC0 6 white step  
4 PTB11 0 yellow dir       4  PTC9 6 yellow dir
5 PTC10 1 white step       5  PTC8 7 white step
6 PTC11 1 yellow dir       6  PTC1 7 yellow dir
